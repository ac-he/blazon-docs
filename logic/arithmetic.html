Do Arithmetic With Two Variables

Per pall [tincture 1], [tincture 2] and [tincture 3]; in chief [charge 1], to dexter [charge 2], to sinister [charge 3].

or

Per pall [tincture 1], [tincture 2] and [tincture 2]; to dexter [charge 2], to sinister [charge 3].

Example 1

Per pall argent, sable, and azure; in chief an anchor, to dexter a scallop, to sinister two ships.

Add Variable13225 to Variable46.

Example 2

Per pall argent, sable, and azure; to dexter a scallop, to sinister two ships.

Add Variable13225 to Variable46.
