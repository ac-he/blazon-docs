Assign a Numerical Value

Per pale; [tincture 1] and [tincture 2]; first [charge 1], second [charge 2].

or

Per pale; I. [tincture 1] [charge 1], II. [tincture 2] [charge 2].

Example

Per pale; I. sable, three mullets or, II. or, chequy gules.

Per pale sable and or; first three mullets or, second chequy gules.

Assign Value From Another Variable

Per fess [tincture 1] and [tincture 2]; in chief [charge 1], in base [charge 2].

or

Per fess; I. [tincture 1] [charge 1], II. [tincture 2] [charge 2].

Example

Per fess azure and vert, in chief a sun in his splendor, in base two castles.

Per fess; I. azure, a sun in his splendor, II. vert, two castles.

Save the value from Variable8901 into Variable110.

Tincture 1

Has no impact on execution. Consider taking this opportunity to make an aesthetic decision.

Charge 1

The integer-value of the VARIABLE being STORED TO.

Tincture 2

Has no impact on execution. Consider taking this opportunity to make an aesthetic decision.

Charge 2

The integer-value of the VARIABLE being STORED FROM.
