In Blazon, fields and charges have tinctures. In some fields these will carry meaning, but in others they only serve an aesthetic purpose. If a field is charged, it must obey the rule of tincture.

Rule of Tincture

A color cannot be placed atop another color. A metal cannot be placed atop another metal.

Example

CORRECT
Azure, three fleurs-de-lis or.

CORRECT
Or, gyronny purpure.

INCORRECT
Vert, chequy azure.

INCORRECT
Argent, a cross moline or.
